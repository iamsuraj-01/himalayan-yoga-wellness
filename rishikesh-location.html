Rishikesh

Rishikesh, often referred to as the "Yoga Capital of the World," is nestled in the foothills of the Himalayas along the sacred Ganges River. Known for its spiritual ambiance and serene surroundings, Rishikesh is an ideal destination for those seeking inner peace, self-discovery, and a deeper connection with nature.

Activities

Rishikesh is not only a spiritual haven but also an adventurer's paradise. Engage in river rafting on the mighty Ganges, participate in the captivating Ganga Aarti, trek to stunning waterfalls, or immerse yourself in yoga and meditation workshops amidst nature. The city offers something for everyone, making it an ideal destination for both thrill-seekers and peace-seekers alike.

  • Trekking to Scenic Waterfalls and Hidden Trails
  • Overnight Camping by the Ganges River
  • Yoga and Meditation Workshops Amidst Nature
  • Participate in the Sacred Ganga Aarti Ceremony

Accommodation

Stay in the serene settings of Rishikesh, where a variety of accommodation options await you. From cozy ashrams to boutique hotels, each place offers a peaceful environment conducive to relaxation and self-discovery. Enjoy the comfort of your stay while being surrounded by the breathtaking views of the Himalayas and the sacred Ganges River.

Getting There

Rishikesh is well-connected by road, rail, and air. The nearest airport is the Jolly Grant Airport in Dehradun, just 35 km away. Regular bus services and private taxis are available from major cities like Delhi, Haridwar, and Dehradun.
